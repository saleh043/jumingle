🔒 Your privacy

Jumingle is anonymous by design. We don't ask for your name or email. Video and audio are sent directly between you and the other person and are never recorded. We store only the minimum needed to keep the service safe (a hashed IP for bans, basic counts). Read our Privacy Policy.
