Support & FAQ

My camera doesn't work

Video chat needs HTTPS and camera permission. Make sure you allowed access in your browser, and that no other app is using the camera. On strict networks a TURN server may be required.

It says I'm blocked

Your access may have been temporarily restricted after a report. Temporary bans expire automatically. If you think this is a mistake, contact us.

How do I report someone?

Press the Report button during a chat and tell us what happened. Our moderators review reports and can ban offenders.

Is it really anonymous?

Yes. No account, no email. We only keep a hashed IP for safety and a few anonymous counters. See our Privacy Policy.
