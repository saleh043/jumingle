About Jumingle

Jumingle is a lightweight, anonymous text and video chat platform inspired by the classic "talk to a stranger" experience. There's no sign-up and no app to install - pick a nickname, choose text or video, and you're instantly connected with someone new from around the world.

Built simple, on purpose

Jumingle runs on a single Node.js server with Socket.IO for realtime messaging and a small SQLite database. Video uses WebRTC, so streams travel directly between browsers and never touch our servers. It's designed to be easy for one developer to host and maintain.

Privacy by design

We don't ask for your name or email. We store only a hashed IP for safety enforcement and a few anonymous counters. Read more in our Privacy Policy.
