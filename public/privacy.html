Jumingle lets you chat with random strangers without creating an account. We collect as little as possible.

What we store

We store a one-way hash of your IP address (never the raw IP) so we can enforce bans and keep the platform safe. We keep simple anonymous counters (visits, chats started/completed, reports). Your nickname lives only in your browser for the current session.

Video and text content

Video and audio are sent directly between you and the other person (peer-to-peer) and are not recorded by us. Text messages pass through our server only to be delivered and are not written to disk. If you report someone, the last few messages of that conversation are held briefly in server memory so a moderator can review them, then discarded.

Your safety

Never share personal information (full name, address, phone, financial details). You must be 18 or older to use Jumingle.
