Jumingle lets you chat with random strangers without creating an account. We try to collect as little as possible.

What we store

We temporarily store your IP address to operate the service, enforce bans, and keep the platform safe. We log a random temporary nickname and connection time. Reports you submit are stored so moderators can review them.

Video and text content

Video and audio are sent directly between you and the other person (peer-to-peer) and are not recorded by us. Text messages pass through our server only to be delivered and are not permanently stored.

Your safety

Never share personal information (full name, address, phone, financial details). You must be 18 or older to use Jumingle.
